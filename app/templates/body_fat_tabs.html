The body fat percentage equation was developed at the US Naval Health Research Center (NHRC), San Diego, California.

For men, it looks like this:

%BF = 495 / ( 1.0324 - 0.19077 * log10( waist_cm - neck_cm ) + 0.15456 * log10( height_cm ) ) - 450

And for women:

%BF = 495 / ( 1.29579 - 0.35004 * log10( waist_cm + hip_cm - neck_cm ) + 0.22100 * log10( height_cm ) ) - 450

The formulas presented above are for measurements in metric units.
